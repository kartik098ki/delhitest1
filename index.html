About RailQuick

RailQuick: Your Train Seat, One Click Away from Essentials

Train journeys just got smarter—order medicines, snacks, or anything via PNR in minutes. From a real traveler's pain to your hassle-free ride.

Our Story

Kartik Guleria, founder, was traveling to Shirdi when his mom pointed out the gap: no quick delivery for medicines or essentials on trains, unlike 10-min urban services. Research confirmed no app existed—food apps stopped at trains, ignoring everyday needs. After deep thinking, Kartik shared the vision with friend Harshit Sinha; now they're building RailQuick together, turning that journey frustration into seamless seat-side delivery.

Mission & Values

Mission: Empower travelers to enjoy journeys fully—hassle-free, with one-click delivery of anything to their seat, anytime. RailQuick innovates rail logistics by blending quick commerce with train tracking for ultra-fast, precise drops.

Core Values:

  • Passenger-First Hustle: Prioritize speed and reliability over everything.
  • Innovate Boldly: Challenge "train food only" norms with full essentials.
  • Build Together: From two founders to a growing family, learning through action.
